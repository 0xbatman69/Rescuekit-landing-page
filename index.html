How the rescue works

The whole problem is speed. Here's how we win the race.

The instant a wallet is compromised, bots try to sweep out anything of value. A normal rescue is too slow — it happens in steps, and there's a gap between them where a bot can jump in. This tool closes that gap.

Protection that never sleeps

It keeps watch, so you don't have to.

Sometimes a compromised wallet keeps receiving money even after it's been hacked — a refund, a payment, a deposit you forgot about. Each time, the bots get another chance.

Once you register a compromised wallet, this tool keeps an eye on it around the clock. The instant new funds arrive, it steps in and moves them to your safe wallet on its own. You don't need to be online, and you don't need to press a button every time.

Frequently asked questions

Can this always recover 100% of my funds?

There's no guarantee. How much can be recovered depends on how quickly the rescue begins and on network conditions at that moment. The tool is built to give you the best possible chance of getting funds out ahead of the bots — but it can't promise a specific outcome every time.

What is sponsor wallet, and why do i need one?

A sponsor wallet is a separate wallet you control, used only to pay gas fees. Instead of putting money into the compromised wallet (which bots are watching), you fund the sponsor wallet — it covers the transaction fees so the compromised wallet never has to receive anything. That's what removes the window bots normally exploit: there's no funding transaction on the compromised wallet for them to react to.

How does this manage to move funds before bots react?

Instead of moving your funds in several separate steps, everything is bundled together and happens in a single, atomic transaction using EIP-7702. Because there's no pause between steps, there's no window of time for a bot to jump in and grab anything mid-way.

Is my private key stored anywhere, or sent to a server?

Your compromised wallet's private key never leaves your browser. It's used locally, on your device, every time — never sent to us, never stored, never touched by our servers. No exceptions. The only key we ever store is the sponsor wallet's key (a separate wallet that pays gas fees), and only if you explicitly enable autonomous rescues. This is never the default, and it is never your compromised wallet's key.

Do you take a fee from the funds I rescue?

No. There's currently no fee charged on rescued funds — 100% of what's recovered goes straight to your safe wallet.

Which blockchain networks does this support right now?

Right now it works on Monad network, with more planned for the future. As new networks become available, you'll be able to protect wallets across more of them.

Can this rescue NFTs too, not just tokens?

Not yet. Today only Token Rescue is live. NFT Rescue is a separate tool that's coming soon — you'll find it in the Rescue tools section above.
